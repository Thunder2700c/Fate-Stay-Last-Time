The Story

Fuyuki City. A war fought in shadows, where seven Masters summon seven Servants — heroic spirits from across history — to battle for the Holy Grail.

Shirou Emiya, a boy who dreams of being a hero, is pulled into this war against his will. What he finds is not glory, but the weight of ideals that may have been flawed from the start.

This is that story — rewritten.
