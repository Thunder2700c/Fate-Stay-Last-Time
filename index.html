Fate/Stay Last Time

Synopsis

A single broken shoe at a wedding. A viral video that burned a man's dignity to ash. A summoning circle drawn in fury. Two legendary Servants called forth to wage war — not against other Masters, but against an entire world that chose to laugh.

This is the story of Gurtej — a Master who decided that a timeline built on cruelty deserves to be pruned from the Tree of Time.
