Synopsis

A single broken shoe at a wedding. A viral video watched by a billion laughing eyes. A summoning circle drawn in blood and fury. Two legendary Servants called forth — not to fight other Masters, but to burn a world that chose to laugh.

This is the story of Gurtej — a Master without Command Seals, a mediator turned warlord, and the man the internet named Vardilord. His mission: prune this corrupted timeline from the Tree of Time.

But the deeper he descends into war, the more the line between righteous fury and wounded ego begins to blur.
